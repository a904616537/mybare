    • Become a MYbarre Instructor

    Learn how to inspire, motivate, and lead others safely and effectively through the MYbarre program with our 3 day comprehensive Instructor Training. This is an open learning environment delivered in person by our MYbarre Master Trainers. It includes a mix of practical master classes, theory workshops, small group exercises and class teaching practice presentations.

    • The course will cover:

  • Teaching correct body alignment and postures
  • The basic elements of ballet,Pilates and fitness training covered in a MYbarre Class
  • The importance of music,beat,flow and voice in teaching
  • Basic moves and choreography breakdown for all sections of class
  • Using MYbarre props and studio equipment
  • How to format and design a MYbarre Class
  • Teaching advancements,modifications and giving corrections.
