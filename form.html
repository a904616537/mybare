Agreement Objective

At your MYbarre Instructor training you will be required to sign a confidentiality,noncompete and brand usage agreement. In submitting your application form and making payment for the course you agree to the following principles:

The information you recive (manuals, website access and digital media) before and on the course is to be classed as confidential and not to be shared, anyone teaching MYbarre must partake in the course, pass exam and pay the MYbarre instructor licence fee to have this information.

That after completion of the MYbarre course and exam you will pay annual membership to become a MYbarre Licenced Instructor, and as a current MYbarre Licenced Instructor you must use MYbarre format, choreography and principles in all MYbarre classes.

You will adhere to brand guidlelines, in that your classes may only be marketed and named as MYbarre (or any other MYbarre programme that becomes available) you cannot teach any other barre fitness programme under any other name/brand while you are a MYbarre Licenced Instructor.

The full document will be included in your training manual and once signed will be submitted to the MYbarre Master Trainer.
